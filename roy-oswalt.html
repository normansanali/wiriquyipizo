Roy Oswalt Biography

Roy Oswalt is an American former Professional Baseball Pitcher who was born and brought up in Weir, Mississippi. He is widely known for playing for the majority of his Major League Baseball (MLB) career with the ‘Houston Astros’. He is also known as a three-time All-Star, which he was selected from 2005 to 2007. Roy also played for other major baseball teams including the Philadelphia Phillies (2010–2011), Texas Rangers (2012), and Colorado Rockies (2013).

Roy began playing for Astros after they selected him in the 1996 MLB draft and made his MLB debut with the Houston Astros in 2001, where he finished his rookie season with a win-loss record of 14–3. He represented the Astros in their first World Series appearance in 2005, where he was profiled as the Most Valuable Player of the 2005 National League Championship Series (NLCS).
