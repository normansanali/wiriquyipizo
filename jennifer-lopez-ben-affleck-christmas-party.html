Inside Jennifer Lopez and Ben Afflecks Star-Studded Christmas Party

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  322 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Christmas spirit is alive and well at the Affleck house.

This weekend, Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck threw a lavish holiday bash at their Hollywood home, welcoming a star-studded guest list.

Famous friends in attendance reportedly included Kim Kardashian, Doja Cat, Billie Eilish, and Eric André. Jane Fonda was also there, per a clip shared by a guest.

Partygoers shared photos and videos on social media from the party, which showed the Afflecks' stunning festive decorations, including a tent-like light display, a Christmas tree, and a piano, where Lopez, Affleck, and their guests gave intimate musical performances.

Lopez sweetly stood by her husband as he sang John Legend's "By Christmas Eve" to cheering attendees.

The "On the Floor" singer also performed for her friends, looking every bit a pop sensation in a long-sleeved sequined gold gown, matching earrings, and a curly ponytail.

Apart from karaoke with the Afflecks, guests got to enjoy a pizza station, an outdoor bar, and a hot cocoa station. Lopez and Affleck also displayed a gingerbread house decorated with their names and those of their kids, as well as the greeting, "Happy Holidays" from "The Affleck Family."

Lopez and Affleck have been enjoying the holiday season so far. Earlier this month they were seen happily shopping for a Christmas tree with their children.

This year will mark the pair's first Christmas as a married couple, and with their blended family. Lopez and Affleck married in July in a secret Las Vegas chapel wedding, and later in August, in a lavish ceremony at Affleck's Georgia estate.
