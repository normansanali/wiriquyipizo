Michael Darby and Ashely Darby have split! What's the reason behind their divorce?

Ashley has stated that she and her husband are legally separated and intend to divorce after nearly eight years of marriage. According to a source close to the couple, a breakdown of trust between Ashley and Michael was indeed the tipping point for Ashley after years of infidelity suspicions.

Despite being beside Micheal through the storms and defending all the allegations that came up, Ashley revealed that it was too much for her to take in. Many fans of the show were not surprised when Ashley disclosed her and Michael's breakup in a statement to Bravo's Daily Dish. From the start, the marriage has had issues, including their significant age gap, cultural barriers, and, of course, adultery. Ashley also revealed to People that trust issues and inconsistency led her to give up on the relationship.

In her final statement, Ashley said,"Pieces of all these may have affected our pure love for each other, but no one reason is the root cause of our mutual decision to go our separate ways," heavy heartedly she continued," We are now both at very different stages in our lives and have different goals for our futures.
