Grammy award-winning singer/songwriter, Tori Kelly, passed out while she was having dinner with her friends in downtown Los Angeles on the night of July 23rd after saying that her heart was beating fast. She was transferred to Cedars-Sinai Hospital where is currently being treated.

While Tori’s husband, Andre Murillo, is in the hospital taking care of his wife, let’s get to know Tori’s parents up close.

Who Are Tori Kelly Parents?

30-year-old Tori Kelly first gained traction when she made it through to Hollywood Week on the 9th season of American Idol in 2010. Later, in 2015, Kelly’s debut studio album Unbreakable Smile peaked at No. 2 on the Billboard 200 chart in the United States.

California native was nominated for Best New Artist at the 58th Grammy Awards. In 2018, Tori’s second studio album Hiding Place peaked at number 35 on the Billboard 200 chart. The album also received two Grammy Awards, Best Gospel Album, and Best Gospel Performance/Song.

Tori released her third studio album Inspired by True Events and fourth album A Tori Kelly Christmas in 2019 and 2020 respectively.

I Was Made For Loving You singer has always been open about the influence her parents had on her music. While promoting her first EP “Handmade Songs by Tori Kelly,” Tori opened up about her upbringing and the influence it had on her musical journey.

“My parents were always playing different styles growing up. Everything from Led Zeppelin to a lot of Lenny Kravitz, and then you got the soul stuff like Maxwell [and] Jill Scott… I give them a lot of credit for just playing all different genres, and no genre was off limits, so it’s kind of all in me somewhere,” Tori told Fuse.

Tori’s third studio album “Inspired by True Events,” which the award-winning singer herself deemed her “most vulnerable album to date”, takes its inspiration from her parent’s divorce.

Talking about the album with Forbes, Tori told that she never wanted to make “Inspired by True Events” such a personal record but it just so happened. “It wasn’t intentional… But there was just so much that went on in the last couple of years… My parents separated… At one point, but not right away, that all went into the music.”
