Lizbeth Meredith- All About The Author Of Stolen by Their Father

 Posted on September 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  389 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Stolen by Their Father, a new horror film from Lifetime will debut this weekend. It is based on author Lizbeth Meredith’s book and focuses on actual events from her life. The children were abducted by their father and Meredith’s violent ex-husband, who threatened to murder her if she made any mistakes. The film’s plot revolved around Meredith, who went to any length to get her girls back home.

“Pieces of Me: Rescuing My Kidnapped Daughters” was the title of the author’s memoir. According to Good Reads, the Amazon best-seller book is described as follows:

“Pieces of Me” tells the story of Lizbeth’s quest to return her internationally stolen kids from Greece to Alaska. It’s the tale of a 29-year-old lady whose life had been marred by family violence and childhood abduction, and who then confronted her own $100,000 issue on a $10-an-hour budget.”

It continues, ”

“It’s the tale of a kind community in Anchorage, Alaska, and a welcoming community in Greece that helped Lizbeth make the unthinkable a reality.”

All About Lizbeth Meredith

Lizbeth Meredith is a Tennessee novelist who lives in Chattanooga. She is also a public speaker, podcaster, and internet marketing instructor. Meredith has a bachelor’s degree in journalism and a master’s in psychology. She’s also been a child abuse investigator, a domestic violence advocate, and a retired juvenile probation supervisor. She was the daughter of two high school dropouts who divorced before she was born.

As a youngster, Meredith was subjected to child maltreatment, parental abduction, poverty, and sibling separation. She met a US citizen from Greece in her twenties, married him, and dreamed of creating a satisfying life. Her dreams quickly changed to nightmares as she found herself in a domestic shelter with her two kids. From mental trauma to poverty, she overcame every challenge to protect her children and alter her life narrative.

When will the premiere of Stolen by Their Father take place?

Meredith will be portrayed by Grey’s Anatomy actress Sarah Drew, while Kimonos Kouris will play her ex-husband. Stolen by Their Father, directed by Simone Stock, will debut on Lifetime on Saturday, March 5 at 8 p.m. Eastern Time (ET). Those without access to the channel may use one of many streaming services, including FuboTV, Philo, DirecTV Stream, Sling, and YouTube TV.
