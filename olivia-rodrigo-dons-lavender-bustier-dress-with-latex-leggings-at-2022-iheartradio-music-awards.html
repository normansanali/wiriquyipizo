Olivia Rodrigo dons lavender bustier dress with latex leggings at 2022 iHeartRadio Music Awards

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  568 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Olivia Rodrigo had a victory-filled evening at the 2022 iHeartRadio Music Awards in Los Angeles. 

The singer-songwriter, 19, walked the red carpet in low-cut, off-the-shoulder lavender dress that pushed up her cleavage. 

Her top’s color seemed to match the background of her hit album Sour which came out just over one year ago.  

A night to remember: Olivia Rodrigo had a victory-filled evening at the 2022 iHeartRadio Music Awards in Los Angeles

On her lower half she added skin tight latex leggings and a pair of black combat boots. 

Rodrigo’s hair was parted in the middle and pulled back into a tight ponytail. 

The drivers license singer also posed with the three awards she took home from the event: Female Artist of the Year, Best New Pop Artist, and TikTok Bop of the Year.

While inside the event, Rodrigo posed with many of her fellow nominees and music peers including Old Town Road rapper and winner of Male Artist of the Year at the event Lil Nas X, 22, who wore an all black ensemble complete with a pair of leather gloves. 

She also took a photo next to Halsey, 27, whose silver rhinestone jumpsuit left little to the imagination, only covering the bare minimum of her torso.   

Rodrigo posed with actress Iris Apatow, 19, reality star Bethenny Frankel, 51, and Frankel’s daughter Bryn Hoppy, 11.  

The good 4 u singer gave several speeches from the stage, and she accepted her award for Female Artist of the Year from legendary singer-songwriter Avril Lavigne.

Perhaps the results of the iHeartRadio Music Awards are a sign of things to come for the 19-year-old phenom who is nominated for seven Grammys at this year’s ceremony on April 3.   

The iHeartRadio Music Awards is an annual ceremony honoring ‘the most-played artists and songs on iHeartRadio stations and the iHeartRadio app throughout 2021’ according to the event’s website.

Now in its ninth year, the show has featured a number of incredible performers in its past including Maroon 5, Camila Cabello, Bruno Mars, Taylor Swift and many more. 

This year’s ceremony will featured performances by John Legend, Megan Thee Stallion, Jason Aldean and Jennifer Lopez, who also received The Icon Award at the event.
