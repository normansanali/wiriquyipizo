Devin Booker leaves thirsty comment on Kendall Jenners bikini photo

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  152 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kendall Jenner and Devin Booker’s romance is heating up.

Jenner, 25, posted a series of bikini photos on Instagram this week while vacationing at a luxe villa in Mexico, and her 24-year-old beau was left so impressed he re-shared the images on his own Instagram story.

“Whew,” Booker wrote above the photos, adding a sweating emoji alongside one of the snaps.

The Phoenix Suns player and Jenner first made headlines for their rumored romance over the summer, when they started getting flirty on Instagram.

They were then spotted out together various times, further fueling the rumors that they were dating.

Following Justin Bieber’s star-studded party for Justine Skye in August, spies spotted Jenner and Booker grabbing breakfast together with Booker’s dog, Haven.

By October, the couple was still together, though a source told Us Weekly they were “just having fun.”

Apparently, they’re still having fun.
