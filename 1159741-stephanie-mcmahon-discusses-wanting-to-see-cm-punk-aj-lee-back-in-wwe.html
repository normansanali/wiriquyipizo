Stephanie McMahon Discusses Wanting To See CM Punk &amp; AJ Lee Back In WWE

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  133 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Stephanie McMahon recently did an interview with The Metro about her desire to see CM Punk and AJ Lee back in WWE. 

Lee retired from the company a few years ago and has stated that she doesn’t have an interest in wrestling again. 

As for Punk, he hasn’t wrestled since 2014 but is under contract with Fox Sports to work as an analyst for WWE Backstage. Here is what McMahon had to say: 

‘I think Punk’s been pretty vocal that he’s not interested in an in-ring return right now, but for sure, that would be interesting. 

And I would love to see AJ Lee back in the women’s division. I think AJ Lee was an incredibly compelling character,’ she said. ‘You know, I’m a big fan of AJ Lee’s, I really am.’
