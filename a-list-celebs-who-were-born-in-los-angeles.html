10 A-List Celebs Who Were Born In Los Angeles

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  933 words  |  Larita Shotwell

It's no secret that Los Angeles is one of the best places for actors, musicians, models, and reality television stars to be as that's where the entertainment industry is pretty much based. Today's list takes a look at those celebs who were actually born in the City of Angels which presumably made their path towards fame easier.

From Hollywood stars such as Jennifer Aniston and Angelina Jolie, over famous siblings like the Kardashian-Jenners and the Hadids, to musicians like Billie Eilish — keep on scrolling to see just who made the list!

10 Billie Eilish

Kicking the list off is musician Billie Eilish who was born on December 18, 2001, in Los Angeles, California. The popular singer — who recently released her tell-all documentary Billie Eilish: The World’s A Little Blurry — rose to fame in 2019 after releasing her debut studio album When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?. Ever since then, Billie has been an impressive young voice in the music industry who is bound to have an incredibly successful career.

9 The Kardashian-Jenner Sisters

Let's move on to the Kardashian-Jenner sisters who are, yes — all born in Los Angeles. Kourtney, Kim, Khloe, Kendall, and Kylie rose to fame in 2007 once the popular reality television show Keeping Up With the Kardashians premiered. Ever since then, the ladies expanded their fortune by dabbling into different career paths such as modeling, launching beauty and clothing lines, and plenty more.

8 Jennifer Aniston

Next on the list is Hollywood star Jennifer Aniston who was born on February 11, 1969, in the Sherman Oaks neighborhood of Los Angeles. Jennifer rose to fame in the 90s as Rachel Green on the popular sitcom Friends and ever since the actress has starred in a multitude of blockbusters.

Today, Jennifer Aniston is one of the most successful actresses of her generation and some of her most famous movies include Marley & Me, Horrible Bosses, and We're the Millers.

7 Angelina Jolie

Speaking of famous actresses — let's move on to Angelina Jolie who was born on June 4, 1975, in Los Angeles, California. The Hollywood star rose to fame in the 90s with roles in movies like Hackers, George Wallace, Gia, and Girl, Interrupted — and she, later on, starred in blockbusters such as Lara Croft: Tomb Raider, Mr. & Mrs. Smith, A Mighty Heart, and Maleficent.

6 Kristen Stewart

Another famous actress that is actually from Los Angeles is Kristen Stewart. Kristen was born on April 9, 1990, in Los Angeles, California — and she rose to fame for her portrayal of Bella Swan in the popular Twilight Saga. Since then, Kristen went on to star in quite a few famous movies and some of her most notable ones include Snow White and the Huntsman, Still Alice, Charlie's Angels, and Underwater.

5 Blake Lively

Let's move on to actress Blake Lively who was born on August 25, 1987, in the Tarzana neighborhood of Los Angeles, California. Blake rose to fame by starring in the movie The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants and its sequel — as well as for portraying Serena van der Woodsen on the popular teen drama Gossip Girl. Since then, Blake — who is married to Canadian actor Ryan Reynolds — has starred in plenty of famous movies like The Age of Adaline, The Shallows, All I See Is You, and A Simple Favor.

4 Gigi And Bella Hadid

The Kardashian-Jenners are not the only famous sisters that made it onto today's list — models Gigi and Bella Hadid were also born in Los Angeles. The two sisters rose to fame in 2014 when their modeling careers kicked off.

Since then, both Gigi and Bella have walked plenty of famous runways and graced countless magazine covers all across the globe. Today, the two ladies are definitely some of the most successful models in the industry.

3 Jake Gyllenhaal

Next on the list is Hollywood star Jake Gyllenhaal who was born on December 19, 1980, in Los Angeles, California. The famous actor rose to fame in the late 90s and early 2000s with roles in movies like October Sky, A Dangerous Woman, Homegrown, and Donnie Darko. Since then, Jake established himself as a staple in the movie industry and he starred in plenty of critically acclaimed movies like Brokeback Mountain, Nocturnal Animals, and Nightcrawler.

2 Mary-Kate And Ashley Olsen

Teen icons Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen are also from Los Angeles as the two twins fraternal twins were born on June 13, 1986, in Sherman Oaks, California. The fraternal twins rose to fame as infants because of their portrayal of Michelle Tanner on the television show Full House in the late 80s. All throughout the 90s and early 2000s, Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen released plenty of iconic teen movies, and even though the two quit acting since — they're still pretty famous stars!

1 Gwyneth Paltrow

Wrapping the list up is actress Gwyneth Paltrow who was born on September 27, 1972, in Los Angeles, California. Gwyneth rose to fame in the 90s with roles in movies such as Seven, Emma, Sliding Doors, and A Perfect Murder — and she has since established herself as a serious actress in the movie industry. Over the years, Gwyneth Paltrow has starred in countless famous movies some of which are The Talented Mr. Ripley, The Royal Tenenbaums, and Contagion.
