Allison McAtee Bloomington

Bloomington is a 2010 coming-of-age drama movie about a former child actress (Sarah Stouffer) attending college in search of independence and who ends up becoming romantically involved with a professor, played by Allison McAtee. Their relationship thrives until an opportunity to return to acting forces them to make life-altering decisions.

Bloomington was written and directed by Fernanda Cardoso as well as filmed in Indianapolis, Indiana, and Los Angeles, California.

Allison McAtee Education And Career

McAtee studied Theatre at the University of Pittsburgh and Imperial College in London, and start her career appearing in stage productions Off-Broadway in NYC.

Allison made her television debut on Rescue Me (TV series) quickly followed by ‘’Hope and Faith’’ in the year 2005.

Allison later guest-starred on Ugly Betty, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation and its spin-offs CSI: Miami and CSI: NY, and well as Castle, ‘’The Mentalist’’, NCIS, and Revenge.

She was also a recurring guest star for two seasons on showtime’s Californication. In the film, she has appeared in The Killing Floor (in the year 2007) and Hell Ride (in the year 2008), and ‘’Iron Man’’ (in the year 2008) before landing the leading role in the independent drama Bloomington (in the year 2010).

In the year 2014, she joined the cast of the Oprah Winfrey Network primetime drama, The Haves, and the Have Nots.

Allison plays Maggie Day, campaign manager to Jim Cryer (John Schneider) and David Harrington (Peter Parros). Allison was promoted to regular cast members as of the third season.
