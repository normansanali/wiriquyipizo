What Happened to Mackenzie Ziegler from Dance Moms?

 Posted on September 2, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  796 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Mackenzie Ziegler, born in 2004, rose to fame as a child star through her appearances on the reality dance show Dance Moms alongside her older sister, Maddie Ziegler.

Since her time on the show, Mackenzie has ventured into various avenues, including music, acting, modeling, and entrepreneurship, solidifying her status as a versatile entertainer and a well-rounded personality.

Musical Career 

Debut Album and Early Success

In 2014, Mackenzie released her debut album, Mack Z, which achieved significant success, peaking at #1 on the iTunes pop charts and #7 on iTunes' albums sales chart across all genres.

The music video for the album's lead single, "Girl Party," also reached #1 on the iTunes pop chart and garnered over 80 million views.

Throughout 2015 and 2016, she continued to release singles and videos under the name Mack Z, showcasing her musical talents and establishing herself as a promising young artist.

Sophomore Album and Collaboration with Johnny Orlando

In 2016, Mackenzie rebranded herself and released her first single under her full name, a duet with singer Johnny Orlando titled "Day & Night."

The accompanying music video garnered over 24 million views and marked a new chapter in her music career. In 2018, she released her second studio album, Phases, further demonstrating her growth as an artist.

Notably, she also collaborated with award-winning singer Sia, who wrote one of the singles, "Wonderful," from her sophomore album.

Literary and Cosmetics Ventures

Mackenzie expanded her influence beyond music by authoring a book titled Kenzie's Rules for Life: How to be Happy, Healthy, and Dance to Your Own Beat in 2018.

The book, aimed at children, garnered success and ranked No. 7 on the Publishers Weekly best-seller list for juvenile non-fiction. Additionally, in the same year, she launched her own cosmetics line, "Love, Kenzie," showcasing her entrepreneurial spirit and diverse interests.

Acting and Television Appearances

Transition to Acting

Mackenzie made her acting debut on Nickelodeon's sitcom Nicky, Ricky, Dicky& Dawn in 2015, marking the beginning of her transition to the screen. Her foray into acting allowed her to showcase her versatility and expand her presence in the entertainment industry.

Television Competitions and Stage Performances

Her participation in the junior edition of ABC's Dancing with the Stars showcased her dancing prowess and culminated in reaching the finals of the competition.

Notably, she danced with her sister for the first time on television since their Dance Moms days during one of the episodes, marking a significant moment in her career.

Mackenzie also starred as Dorothy in a holiday stage pantomime adaptation, The Wonderful Winter of Oz, in Pasadena, California, adding to her list of notable performances.

Modeling and Brand Endorsements

Mackenzie's talents and influence extended to the modeling industry, where she walked the Polo Ralph Lauren Runway in New York City and appeared in an ad campaign video for Glamour Magazine.

Her sizable following on social media also attracted lucrative brand endorsement deals, including collaborations with Clean & Clear and Justice, a clothing retailer targeting the tween girl market.

Despite the challenges faced by Justice, Mackenzie's successful brand partnerships showcased her appeal and influence in the industry.

Notable Achievements

Touring and International Workshops

Mackenzie's global presence was evident through her Day & Night Tour with Johnny Orlando, which spanned North America and the UK, showcasing her international appeal.

She and her sister also embarked on dance workshop tours in Australia and New Zealand, further solidifying their global influence.

Television Appearances and Competition Shows

Her appearances on The Masked Dancer as "Tulip," where she secured the third-place position, and her role as a guest judge on the Quibi dance competition show Floored highlighted her enduring presence in the television industry. Her duet with Sia on the song "Exhale" and her involvement in the animated film Ice Princess Lily demonstrated her versatility and continued success in the entertainment industry.

Personal Growth and Inspirational Influence

Social Media Presence and Inspirational Messaging

Mackenzie's substantial social media following of 8 million on Instagram reflects her ability to connect with a diverse audience.

Her positive and inspirational messaging resonates with her fans, embodying a role model for young individuals navigating life's challenges.

Her book, Kenzie's Rules for Life, further underscores her commitment to inspiring and uplifting her young audience.

Sisterly Bond and Personal Reflection

Throughout her journey, Mackenzie acknowledged the influence of her sister, Maddie Ziegler, highlighting the importance of their bond and mutual support. Her personal growth, from overcoming insecurities to embracing confidence and self-belief, serves as a testament to her resilience and maturity in navigating the entertainment industry.

 

Mackenzie Ziegler's post-Dance Moms journey is a testament to her multifaceted talents, resilience, and enduring impact on the entertainment industry.

From her musical accomplishments and acting ventures to her inspirational messaging and global influence, Mackenzie continues to captivate audiences worldwide.
