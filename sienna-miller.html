Name:Sienna Miller

Hometown:New York, New York

Birthday:December 28, 1981

About

Sienna Miller was born in New York but moved to England as a child. She returned to New York to study at the Lee Strasberg drama school.

Miller has appeared in popular films such as Alfie, Casanova and 21 Bridges. She also starred on 2022’s Anatomy of a Scandal and 2023’s Extrapolations. In addition to acting, Miller was a model and designer for her fashion label, Twenty8Twelve, which she founded with her sister. They both stepped down as creative directors in 2012.

In the early 2000s, Miller made headlines for her very public relationship with actor Jude Law, who cheated on her with his kids’ nanny. The duo split in 2006 but rekindled their romance in 2009. They split for good in 2011.

The actress later dated Tom Sturridge from 2011 to 2015. The exes share daughter Marlowe, whom they welcomed in 2012. Miller was first linked to boyfriend Oli Green in February 2022. News broke in August 2023 that they are expecting their first baby together.
