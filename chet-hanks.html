Chet Hanks is a popular American Actor and Musician. Chet Hanks is widely known for his character as rapper Blake in the hip-hop musical drama television series titled Empire.

Early Life and Childhood

Chet Hanks was born on August 4, 1990, in the city of Los Angeles, California, United States. His age is 32 years as of 2023 and his full name is Chester Marlon Hanks.

Similarly, he is American by nationality and Christian by religion. Moreover, his zodiac sign is Leo which belongs to the element of Fire, according to astrologers.

He was born to actor Tom Hanks (father) and actress Rita Wilson (mother). Likewise, he has three siblings and they are Colin Hanks, Truman Hanks, and Elizabeth Ann Hanks.

Career and Professional Life

Moving on to his career, his acting career began in 2007 playing the character of Dexter in the film Bratz. After achieving sobriety, he worked in television in the latter half of the decade.

On the American version of Shameless, he played the role of Charlie, the father of Sierra Morton’s child, from 2016 to 2018. Similarly, from 2018 to 2019, he portrayed the character of rapper Blake in the hip-hop drama series titled Empire.

In 2020, he appeared in a World War II film Greyhound starring his father. Later that year, he starred in the Showtime courtroom miniseries titled Your Honor in the role of Joey Maldini, the best friend of a hit-and-run victim’s brother.

Musical Career

He recorded a song titled White and Purple while he was a student at Northwestern University in 2011. The song was a remix of Wiz Khalifa’s single titled Black and Yellow, in which he changed the lyrics to reflect his school colors.

After meeting together in 2016, Hanks and Drew Arthur formed the musical duo named FTRZ. They released two singles “Models and NowhereLand” and the “Ocean Park EP” in 2018. Likewise, the duo has also released two tracks titled “Harley” and “Ticket Out My Head” in 2020.
