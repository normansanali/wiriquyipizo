Dominique Flournoy, sister of Apple Watts, has been updating about her sister’s condition after the 23 March accident. She also launched GoFundMe for her sister’s procedure. So, Apple’s fans wanted to learn more about Dominique who has been very supportive throughout this ordeal.

Before that Dominique also went to support her sister for her appearance on Love & Hip Hop. She wrote on her IG, “Went to support my Lil sis.. Still got a lot to work on but I love her no matter what”.

Below, we’ll cover her age, job, and relationship status.

Meet Dominique Flournoy, Apple Watts Sister

Apple Watts is on a steady recovery as updated by her older sister Dominique Flournoy. They have known been together since they were kids. Dominique has been updating Apple’s conditions following a terrifying car accident in March 2022.

During this ordeal, she updated followers about multiple surgeries which Apple underwent. One of the first surgery was for her broken wrist which required reconstruction. It was followed by neck and spine surgery.

Dominique also warned Apple’s followers of all the fake GoFundMe. Meanwhile, she organized the GoFundMe for Apple on 31 March 2022. In the bio of the GoFundMe, Dominique shared that she received “A call saying that your sister was in a terrible car accident and has been rushed to the trauma center. It’s the call that brings you to your knees, rips out your heart, and causes your mind to go to places you never, ever want to go”.

As of this article, GoFundMe gathered $12,632 of the $35,000 goal.

In the latest post, Dominique shared on her Instagram, “My sister is up and alert she understands and follows commands right now, she is in speech therapy so that her trachea can be removed. Therapy helps with speech and swallowing.”

She added, “She is also in physical therapy. She’s been trying to balance herself by sitting on the edge of the bed with her feet on the floor it’s a process.”

Dominique shared that Apple was still waiting to be transferred back to California.

In June 2018, Dominique hinted at difficult relationship situations between the two. She posted a photo of Apple on her IG and captioned it, “I really miss my Lil sister wish things could be different between us. we still blood IDC”.

Do Dominique Flournoy And Apple Watts Have The Same Parents?

No, Dominique Flournoy and Apple Watts don’t have the same parents.

Dominique’s father is Aaron Flournoy who has appeared a few times on her Instagram. On Father’s Day 2017, she posted on her Facebook, “Happy fathers day to the best dad ever… I love him dearly I look up to him. He wasn’t always there in my life but the times he was made up for it by teaching me how to be a good mother and a woman. I love him and ill do anything for him. I got his back like he got mine”.

Aaron lives in Los Angeles, California. According to his LinkedIn, he owned Bills Bike Shop and also served as a manager.

Several blogs and articles have been written about Aaron. He was also featured on Medium when Bills Bike shop was removed from the USC campus. “I want everyone to know I’m still alive and I’m still fighting on,” said Aaron, who is known in the neighborhood as “Lil Bill”.

Before Aaron, his father Bill owned and operated Bill’s Bike Shop for over 40 years.

Meanwhile, Dominique’s mother is Henri Underwood Joyce. She posted a picture of Henri on Mother’s Day 2018. Dominique recently celebrated her mother’s birthday on 2 June 2022. She wrote, “Happy birthday momma we love you”.

As for Apple, she was on a journey to find her biological father. And her step-father John Watts didn’t have a pleasant relationship.

Dominique Flournoy Age

Born in the year 1983, in June 2022, Dominique Flournoy is 39 years old. She celebrates her birthday on 11 June.

Dominique Flournoy Job
