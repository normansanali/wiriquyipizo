Katelyn Jae career

When she was just three years old, her parents noticed her exceptional singing abilities after buying her a Karaoke machine. As she progressed through these lessons, she began to make a reputation for herself in the industry. Her big break came when she participated in the International Model and Talent Association conference, where she ranked among the top five participants. At this point, it became clear that this rising star had what it took to succeed in music. Her song “Skills” even received radio play during one of these competitions! However, it wasn’t until 2012 that everything changed for her when she won Take The Stage – a highly coveted competition with a grand prize of $20,000.

With this win under her belt and newfound recognition from industry professionals, she signed on with Wright Entertainment Group and worked closely with Johnny Wright himself, who is an established figure within the music world.
