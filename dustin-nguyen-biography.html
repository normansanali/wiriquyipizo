Who is Dustin Nguyen?

Dustin Nguyen is an actor, producer, and martial artist of Vietnamese American nationality who was seen in both TV plus film. Dustin Nguyen is popular for featuring in “Little Fish”, “The Doom Generation”, “The Rebel”, etc.

For his amazing acting in the Vietnamese film GENTLE, he won the Leonardo da Vinci Golden Horse Award for Best Supporting Actor in 2015.

His upcoming screen project Sinking Spring as Ho Dinh is in the post-production stage and it will release soon.

He has written two films Once Upon a Time in Vietnam (2013) and Fool for Love (2010).

Dustin Nguyen – Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His full birthdate is 17 Sep 1972 and he was born in Saigon, South Vietnam as Nguyen Xuan Tri. Similarly, the name of his father is Xuân Phát, and his mother’s identity is My Le.

His mom was an actress/dancer while his dad was an actor/comedian/writer. Further, Dustin Nguyen’s ancestry is Vietnamese and has a brother.

Talking about his education, he first went to Garden Grove High School and then he enrolled at Orange Coast College.

From the latter, he majored in communications but never completed his studies.
