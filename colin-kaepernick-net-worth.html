Colin Rand Kaepernick Wiki Biography

Colin Rand Kaepernick was born on the 3rd November, 1987 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, USA. He is a professional football player who plays in the position of quarterback in the San Francisco 49ers team in the NFL. Colin Kaepernick has been accumulating his net worth as a professional player since 2011.

How rich is this professional quarterback?
