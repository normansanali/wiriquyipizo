Honda Motor Company, Ltd. is a Japanese public multinational conglomerate manufacturer of automobiles founded in Hamamatsu, Japan (October 1946, incorporated 24 September 1948; 73 years ago) by the Soichiro Honda and Takeo Fujisawa.
