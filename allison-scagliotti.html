Career

Scagliotti has appeared in a number of minor television shows, including One Tree Hill, CSI, and the Nickelodeon comedy Zoey 101. Scagliotti’s first recurring role was as Mindy Crenshaw, Josh’s competitor and later girlfriend, on Drake & Josh. From 2004 through 2008, she appeared in seven episodes.

Merry Christmas, Drake & Josh, a spin-off film, saw her reprise her role. Maddie in the 2005 short film Redemption Maddie was her first main role.

In the ninth season of Smallville, she played Jayna, the female Wonder Twin, in the episode “Idol.” On Halloween 2010, she co-hosted SyFy’s Ghost Hunters Live.

Scagliotti is best known for playing Claudia Donovan in the Syfy television series Warehouse 13. Her character debuted in the first season in 2009, and she appeared in the majority of the remaining episodes till 2014. (though credited as a guest star until season two).

When she guest-starred in the season four episode “Crossing Over,” her character crossed over to Eureka. She starred in the independent film Losers Take All, which was set in the 1980s rock music scene and released in 2011.

Scagliotti made her Broadway debut in December 2012, playing Michelle, a struggling actor, in Darren Caulley’s play Unhealthy, which premiered on December 4 at HERE Arts Center in New York City. From 2015 to 2017, she played Camille, the main character, in three seasons of Freeform’s Stitchers. Scagliotti also had a recurring role on The Vampire Diaries as Georgie in the eighth and final season of the program in 2016.
