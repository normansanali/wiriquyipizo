Career

Lisa Kudrow started her career as a comedian and become a member of improv and sketch comedy school The Groundlings with the help of her brother’s close friend and popular comedian Jon Lovitz. After that, she joined a comedian group named Unexpected Company along with her then-boyfriend Conan O’Brien and director Tim Hillman.

After that, she made her first on-screen presence with her appearance in an episode of the sitcom TV series Cheers. After that, she has also worked in the sitcom series Bob but just appeared in only three episodes. She also becomes a part of the popular series Newhart. Her first noted performance on the television screen came out when she portrayed the role of Ursula Buffay in the sitcom series titled Mad About You.

She carried on the same character in the popular television series Friends, in which she portrayed the role of Phoebe Buffay, Ursula’s twin sister. The series become a huge hit and she gained huge recognition and appreciation worldwide. She has received so many popular awards and honors during her tenure on Friends.

Along with this series, she played some important roles in popular comedic and dramatic movies like Romy and Michele’s High School Reunion, Analyze that, Wonderland, The Opposite of Sex, and several others. She has also made some guest appearances in television shows like The Simpsons, Hope and Gloria, King of the Hill, Cougar Town, Scandal, Angie Tribeca, and others.

In the year 2004-05, she has worked as a creator, writer, and executive producer in the HBO series titled The Comeback.

She also owns a production company named Is or Isn’t Entertainment. She also worked in the television series Who Do You Think You Are? as an executive producer.

She also co-created a comedic web series titled Web Therapy in the year 2011. She has also been featured in movies like Easy A Hotel for Dogs, Happy Endings, Neighbors, and Neighbors 2 Sorority Rising. She has also appeared in the popular thriller movie titled The Girl on the Train and will be seen in the upcoming Netflix comedy series Space Force.

Currently, she is working as an executive producer of a syndicated game show titled 25 Words or Less. Throughout her career, Lisa Kudrow has worked in 100 movies and television series in the American film and television industry.

Awards

Lisa Kudrow has won many popular awards and honors throughout her career in the industry. She has received over six nominations for the Primetime Emmy Awards for her incredible work in the television series Friends and won it in the year 1998 for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series.

She has also won the Screen Actors Guild Awards two times in her career along with twelve nominations. She has received the American Comedy Awards along with eight nominations all for her work in the popular series Friends.
