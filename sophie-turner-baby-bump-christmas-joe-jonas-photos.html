Sophie Turner Says She Misses Her Baby Belly &amp; Shares Unseen Pic

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  327 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

It's indisputable that 2020 has been an unprecedented year, especially as the COVID-19 pandemic has meant that many people have been unable to spend the holiday season with their family and friends. While Sophie Turner self-isolates with her husband, Joe Jonas, and their new daughter, Willa, the Game of Thrones actress has been reflecting on what she is missing.

Taking to her Instagram Stories, Turner shared a selfie from earlier this year in which she was still pregnant with baby Willa. In answer to the prompt, "Something you miss," Turner wrote, "MY BELLY." The sweet snap is a reminder of just how much has changed for Turner this year, as the new mom has been adjusting to life with a baby girl.

Instagram

Turner also shared a selfie taken by her husband during the Christmas break. Both Turner and Jonas are wearing Christmas hats in the adorable snap.

Instagram

A report recently suggested that Turner and Jonas might be hoping to expand their family, sooner rather than later. A source reportedly told Us Weekly, "Joe and Sophie are already trying to have another baby ... They are really excited to expand their family. Having the baby has made them really close and want to have a large family together."

And according to Entertainment Tonight, Turner and Jonas have quickly taken to parenthood. "The couple is already obsessed and can't stop gloating about their new addition," a source told the publication. "The couple is taking time to enjoy this special moment and have only shared the news and updates with family and friends. With the pandemic Joe and Sophie have been very cautious about who is around them and their little girl."
