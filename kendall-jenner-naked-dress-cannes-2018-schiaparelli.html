Kendall Jenner Naked Dress Cannes 2018

 Posted on July 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  320 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Kendall Jenner is a fan of freeing the nipple, and she continues to demonstrate her stance at Cannes Film Festival 2018.

Related Story

Following her dramatic appearance at last night's Secret Chopard Party, at which the model wore a completely sheer minidress without a bra underneath, Jenner decided to hit the red carpet wearing another naked dress. Again, she opted to go braless, her nipples clearly visible through the light, white fabric.

While last night's minidress was made out of a gauzy, glittery fabric, today's gown is Schiaparelli Haute Couture, from their Fall/Winter 2017-2018 Collection:

Getty Images

Much like last night's outfit, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star wore only nude underwear underneath the gown. And without a bra on, the top of the dress was completely transparent on the red carpet. She accessorized the look with jewelry by Chopard.

Getty Images

Jenner's elegant and extremely naked Schiaparelli Haute Couture gown is made from white silk tulle with polka dots, and accessorized with a wide belt. With its many tiers, and its wispy fabric, Jenner's look bordered on bridal, but the risqué, naked element of the dress was in keeping with the model's style.

While Jenner opted to remove the sleeves from the dress, the look otherwise remained intact. Here, the gown is shown at Paris Fashion Week on July 3, 2017:

Getty Images

Attending a screening of Girls Of The Sun, Jenner showcased the look, once again proving that when it comes to naked dresses, she wins every prize.

And with several more days of Cannes to go, it's possible Jenner will wear even more naked looks before the festival is over.
