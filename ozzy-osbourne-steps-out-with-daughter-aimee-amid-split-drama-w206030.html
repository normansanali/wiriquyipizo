Ozzy Osbourne Steps Out With Daughter Aimee Amid Split Drama

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  319 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Leaning on her for support? Ozzy Osbourne stepped out with his daughter Aimee Osbourne amid the news that he’s splitting from wife Sharon Osbourne after 33 years of marriage.

Ozzy, 67, and Aimee, 32, were photographed leaving an office building in L.A. on Tuesday, May 10. Aimee has rarely made appearances with her famous family over the years and declined to star on their past MTV series, The Osbournes, but she told The Independent last year that she remains close with her parents.

As previously reported, Sharon, 63, recently kicked the Black Sabbath frontman out of their home. A source told Us Weekly that she thought he was having an affair. She confirmed the breakup on The Talk on Tuesday.

“I’ve been avoiding looking at any pictures or reading anything. I know what’s going on. I don’t need to read about it and see about it because it’s like putting salt in a wound. So I don’t need to,” she told her cohosts Julie Chen, Sara Gilbert, Aisha Tyler and Sheryl Underwood.

“I honestly cannot thank people enough for their backing me. For saying they love me. For everybody here at my home, supporting me, sending me messages of love,” she continued. “I honestly am empowered and I have found this inner strength and I’m like, right, OK, what’s next?”

Sharon also revealed that Ozzy had returned home. “He’s back and I’m out of the house,” she said.

The estranged couple married in 1982 and are parents of Aimee, Kelly, 31, and Jack Osbourne, 30. Jack has yet to react publicly to his parents’ marital trouble. Kelly, however, alluded to the breakup in an Instagram post on Tuesday.
