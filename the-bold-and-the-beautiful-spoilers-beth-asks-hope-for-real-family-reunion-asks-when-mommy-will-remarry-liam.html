The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Beth Asks Hope for Real Family Reunion Asks When Mommy Will

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  395 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers reveal that Beth Spencer (Jordyn Lynn Ariza) recently popped up for some bonding time with Hope Logan (Annika Noelle) and Liam Spencer (Scott Clifton). It felt like a big shift since it’d been a while since B&B fans got any scenes with the “Lope” family unit.

Beth clearly enjoyed hanging out with both Liam and Hope, especially since they agreed to take her to the zoo together.

Liam also vowed to step up more often now that Thomas Forrester (Matthew Atkinson) and Douglas Forrester (Henry Joseph Samiri) are gone.

All of that seems important since Hope only has Beth to dote on now. Hope previously had Douglas as well, but now adopted son is off in Paris with Thomas.

That frees up more attention for Beth, who may grow to love all the extra time she’s getting with both Liam and Hope.

At some point, it’s easy to imagine Beth taking a page out of Douglas’ playbook. Beth might beg Hope to be a “real family” with Mommy and Daddy again.

Perhaps Beth will push for a family reunion and start asking when Hope plans to remarry Liam.

Of course, Hope just admitted to John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan) that she’s wary of tying the knot again with anyone.

Since Brooke Logan (Katherine Kelly Lang) has married so many times over the years, Hope doesn’t want to go down that same road.

However, things could change for Hope once Beth’s staring up at her with hopeful little eyes and pleading with her sweet voice.

That’s particularly true since Hope could start bonding with Liam as well through the extra co-parenting time together.

Finn would like to see Hope work things out with Liam, so is that still a possibility?

There was a time when it felt like the Hope and Liam ship had truly sailed, but it doesn’t feel that way anymore.

Now that door’s starting to open back up, maybe Liam and Hope could indeed get back together somewhere down the line.

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say Beth may hope for updates on some happy family reunion news, so we’ll bring you predictions on what the future holds.
