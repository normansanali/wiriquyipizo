WWE Announces CM Punks Successful Triceps Surgery on 2/5 RAW Episode

 Posted on August 1, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  193 words  |  Larita Shotwell

CM Punk is on the road to recovery after undergoing successful surgery on his triceps, as detailed in a video package that aired on the February 5 episode of Raw. Punk sustained the triceps injury at the Royal Rumble premium live event in January.

In the video package, CM Punk shared insights into his current mindset, acknowledging that he anticipates facing more mental challenges than physical ones during his recovery period. He expressed confidence in making the greatest comeback of all time.

The exact timeline for CM Punk’s return to the ring remains uncertain, and fans are eagerly awaiting updates on his recovery progress. Ringside News will continue to provide updates and share information as it becomes available regarding CM Punk’s return to in-ring competition.

Speaking of CM Punk, Drew McIntyre debuted new t-shirt that is now for sale on WWE Shop that mocks Punk’s dreams being crushed for WrestleMania during the February 5, 2024 episode of RAW.
