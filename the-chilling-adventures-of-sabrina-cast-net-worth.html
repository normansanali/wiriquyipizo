Ranking The Chilling Adventures Of Sabrina Cast By Net Worth

 Posted on September 25, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  968 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sabrina the Teenage Witch was a very famous comic book series from Archie Comics. It was so beloved that it spawned the 1996 television movie of the same name starring Melissa Joan Hart. This led to the sitcom of the same name on ABC (and later the WB) also starring Melissa Joan Hart. This brought the character of Sabrina Spellman to kids everywhere in the nineties and early 2000s.

In 2018, the character would make a comeback, but in a very different way. Netflix premiered the fantasy horror series Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. The show starred Kiernan Shipka in the titular role of Sabrina Spellman. It also starred actors Gavin Leatherwood and Ross Lynch. The show was a far departure from the family-friendly sitcom of before. Now the tone was darker and more serious for mature audiences.

The show definitely gained a large following and led to much interest in the cast. So who is the richest cast member? The answer might shock you.

8 Tati Gabrielle - $1.1 Million

Tati Gabrielle played the role of Prudence on the show. Gabrielle was born in 1996 and she began her career as a child model. She was modeling for stores such as Macy's and Nordstrom. By the time she reached middle school, Gabrielle was accepted into the theater program at Oakland School for the Arts. Her first burst of fame came when she starred on the CW's The 100. In addition to Chilling Adventures of Sabrina, Gabrielle has made appearances in shows like The Thundermans and K.C. Undercover.

7 Gavin Leatherwood - $1.5 Million

Gavin Leatherwood played the role of Nicholas Scratch in Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. Leatherwood was born in 1994 in Oregon, where he was also raised. In 2017, he landed his first major acting role in an episode of NCIS titled, "Pandora's Box, Part 1." Leatherwood has also appeared in shows like Grown-ish and The Sex Lives Of College Girls. In addition to his acting ability, Leatherwood also has talent as a singer and musician. He can play the guitar, ukulele, and piano. Leatherwood has released several of his songs to digital and streaming platforms.

6 Chance Perdomo - $3 Million

Chance Perdomo played the character Ambrose Spellman in the series. Ambrose was romantically linked with the character Luke Chalfant played by Darren Mann. The relationship has been met with praise for its representation of relationships within the LGBTQ+ community. Perdomo grew up in London and auditioned for the role of Jughead in the series Riverdale, before it went to Cole Sprouse. He played the character of Jerome in Killed By My Debt, which was one of his earliest roles.

5 Jaz Sinclair - $3.3 Million

Jaz Sinclair played the role of Rosalind Walker on Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. Sinclair began acting as a child and was a student at Dallas University, She is best known for her role as Angela from the 2015 film Paper Towns. In addition to Sabrina, Sinclair has acted in shows such as The Vampire Diaries and Easy. She has appeared as a guest on two episodes of Masterclass on HBO, one episode of Revolution on NBC, and VlogBrothers. Sinclair also appeared as Tasha Williams in three episodes of Rizzoli & Isles on TNT.

4 Kiernan Shipka - $4 Million

Kiernan Shipka starred as the titular character of Sabrina Spellman in the series. Shipka's career began when she was just a baby with appearances in print advertisements. Her family later moved from Chicago to Los Angeles to help Shipla pursue acting. She made her on-screen debut in 2006 when she appeared in some episodes of the series Monk. Shipka appeared in several television shows before landing the role of Sally Draper in Mad Men. She has also appeared in Land of the Lost, The Edge of the Woods, The Legend of Korra, Sofia the First, and Riverdale.

3 Ross Lynch - $5 Million

Ross Lynch played the role of Harvey in Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. He was born in Littleton, Colorado where he studied guitar, piano, and voice as a child. Lynch was homeschooled growing up and relocated with his family to Los Angeles. Lynch's older brother Riker was pursuing a career in Hollywood. Lynch also started going on auditions and was cast in the Disney Channel series Austin & Ally. He also starred in the Disney Channel original movie Teen Beach Movie and its sequel. Lynch is also a singer and musician. He was part of the band R5, which comprised his siblings and a family friend. After their hiatus, Lynch formed another band with his brother Rocky called The Driver Era.

2 Lachlan Watson - $40 Million

Lachlan Watson played the character Theo Putnam on Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. They were born on April 12, 2001. Watson was born and raised in North Carolina. They made their television debut in the 2013 short film The Ultimate Life. Watson's role on Sabrina came in 2018 during the show's first season. In 2017, they guest starred in two episodes of the series Nashville.

1 Michelle Gomez - $215 Million

Michelle Gomez played the role of Madam Satan on Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. Gomez is Scottish and was born in Glasgow. Gomez was inspired to start acting after seeing the play Kiss Me Kate and toured with the Royal Shakespeare Company. She has been in several television shows, most notably Green Wing and The Book Group. She also starred as Missy/The Master on BBC's Doctor Who from 2014 to 2017. Gomez is married to Jack Davenport and the two had a son in 2010.
